Gurukul Bidhya Sadan Secondary School, established in 4th April 1994, is located in Nayabazar, Pokhara-9, Kaski, Nepal. The school offers education from early childhood development (ECD) up to grade 12, with programs in Science and Management streams. It is affiliated with the National Examination Board (NEB) and is recognized by the Ministry of Education. The institution is managed by experienced academicians and emphasizes both academic excellence and extracurricular activities, promoting discipline and cultural values. Facilities include a library, sports amenities, science and computer labs, and counseling services. The school also offers scholarship schemes for deserving students. In 2018, Acharya Narayan served as the principal of the school. However, specific details about the founders and the current principal are not readily available. Notably, the school won the 2nd Inter-college Band Competition organized by the Pokhara Musical Artist's Association Nepal, highlighting its commitment to fostering talent beyond academics. Gurukul Vidya Sadan secured the title at the second 'Inter-School Band Competition,' surpassing 19 musical groups from 18 schools in Pokhara.The event, organized by the Pokhara Musical Artist's Association Nepal, showcased the musical talents of various schools, with Gurukul Vidya Sadan emerging as the winner.
