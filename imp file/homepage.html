About GRUKUL BIDYA SADAN

ONE OF THE RENOWNED SCHOOLS

Gurukul Bidhya Sadan Secondary School, established in 1994, is located in Nayabazar, Pokhara-9, Kaski, Nepal. The school offers education from early childhood development (ECD) up to grade 12, with programs in Science and Management streams. It is affiliated with the National Examination Board (NEB) and is recognized by the Ministry of Education. The institution is managed by experienced academicians and emphasizes both academic excellence and extracurricular activities, promoting discipline and cultural values. Facilities include a library, sports amenities, science and computer labs, and counseling services. The school also offers scholarship schemes for deserving students.
